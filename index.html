The Rumor With A Life Of Its Own

A computational social science experiment exploring how information changes as it moves through people.

Research Question

A message begins as one thing. After passing through a network of people, what does it become?

The simulation models:

  • Social connections
  • Personality differences
  • Memory distortion
  • Information evolution
